About

Academic direction

I am an undergraduate student in the joint program between Delft University of Technology and Eindhoven University of Technology. My work sits at the intersection of machine learning, scientific computing, and engineering design, with an emphasis on technically grounded, research-oriented questions rather than generic product applications.

I am particularly drawn to problems where modern foundation models can be made useful for scientific understanding, structured reasoning, and design automation in physically constrained domains.
