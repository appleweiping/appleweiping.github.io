# Portfolio v3 design system

The interface is an academic collection catalogue first and an immersive museum second. The static site uses restrained editorial typography and generous negative space; the Cabinet of Inquiry expands the same identity into a classical 3D environment.

## Palette

- **Ivory / paper** form the reading surface and avoid stark white.
- **Ink** carries all primary copy at WCAG AA contrast.
- **Jade** is the primary action and institutional accent.
- **Brass** is reserved for exhibit numbering and museum details.
- **Cinnabar** appears only in seals, status marks, and small moments of emphasis.

## Typography

Instrument Serif gives English display text a museum-catalogue voice. Noto Serif SC provides the corresponding Chinese tone. Inter and Noto Sans SC are used for navigation, metadata, controls, and body copy. Fonts are self-hosted or loaded with resilient fallbacks; content never depends on font JavaScript.

## Layout and interaction

The spacing scale follows a four-pixel base with large editorial intervals. Cards use borders and hierarchy instead of generic glass effects or heavy shadows. Focus rings are always visible, controls meet a 44px preferred touch target, and all motion has a reduced-motion equivalent.

## Cabinet of Inquiry

The 3D environment combines European neoclassical proportions with Chinese scholarly details: arches and axial galleries, moon-gate geometry, walnut lattice screens, jade accents, brass labels, and warm exhibit lighting. Every repository receives the same exhibit geometry and interaction affordances. Search, guided navigation, free navigation, and a complete 2D fallback coexist.
